Lasagna

Description

This recipe is for a delicious homemade lasgna. The recipe originates from my great-great grandmother.

Ingredients

  • tomato sauce
  • noodles
  • ground beef
  • spices
  • cheese

Steps

  1. brown the ground beef
  2. combine the cheese, spices and tomato sauce in a bowl
  3. boil the noodles
  4. combine the ingredients by making layers
